We have introduced what we call “Animation Shortcuts” in ParaView! They enable simply and quickly add an Animation Track without opening the Animation View panel, selecting the source and the corresponding property to animate on the Properties panel.

First, the shortcuts have to be enabled in ParaView Settings -> General -> Animation -> Show Animation Shortcuts. After that, all single element numerical ranged properties will have a play button alongside on the Properties panel. By clicking on it, an animation track is added and the keyframe edition dialog shows up.
