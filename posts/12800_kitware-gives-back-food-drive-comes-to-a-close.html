A very happy ending to Kitware’s food drive supporting two local food pantries.

Many thanks from CAPTAIN-Karyl’s Kupboard and the Inter-Faith Council for Social Services. Donations tend to fall off during the summer months.  The generosity of Kitware’s employees has helped fill the food pantry shelves.  There were many bags of refundable cans and the organizations are very grateful for this monetary donation as well.

Together we have made a difference.  A big thank you from all of the families, especially the children, who will benefit from your act of kindness.
