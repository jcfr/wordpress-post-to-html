In February, Kitware welcomed two new team members to our offices in Clifton Park, NY and Carrboro, NC.

Jamie Snape joined our North Carolina office as an R&D Engineer, where he will be working with our Medical Computing team. Jamie earned an M.Math. degree with honors in mathematics from Collingwood College, University of Durham, an M.Sc. degree in computer science from Worcester College, University of Oxford, and M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.

Charles Weatherford joined the New York office as a Communications Coordinator. He earned a B.S. in Environmental Science, with a concentration in Geography and Satellite Imagery Analysis, from the State University of New York at Albany. Prior to working at Kitware, Charles worked as a Production Underwriter for Farm Family Mutual Insurance.

Kitware is excited to welcome Jamie and Charles to the team! Kitware is still growing and looking for enthusiastic, motivated individuals to join our team. For full details on our open positions or to apply, please visit our employment site at jobs.kitware.com .
