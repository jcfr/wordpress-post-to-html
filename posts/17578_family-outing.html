Yesterday, our headquarters in Clifton Park, New York, had our Winter Family Outing. This year, we went roller skating at Guptil’s Arena in Latham, New York. Guptil’s Arena has the distinction of being the world’s largest indoor roller skating rink. The little ones enjoyed it, and even if they fell to the ground, they bounced right back up. Us adults made sure not to hit the ground, since we do not bounce back as well. Afterwards, some of us enjoyed some ice cream despite the 21 degree weather.
