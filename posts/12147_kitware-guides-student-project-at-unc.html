Patrick Reynolds and others in Kitware’s North Carolina office are guiding a student project for the University of North Carolina’s CS 523, a course on software engineering.

Patrick is meeting weekly with a team of student programmers as they experience the challenges and rewards associated with moving a software project from early concepts to specification, implementation, testing and delivery. Patrick acts as educator, consultant, and customer for the student team. The team’s chose to create innovative web-based tools for sharing and annotating medical images for this project. They are using popular photo sharing, blog, and consumer review websites for inspiration.
