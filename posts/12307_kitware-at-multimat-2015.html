The seventh International Conference on Numerical Methods for Multi-Material Fluid Flow ( MultiMat 2015 ) was held this week (September 7-11 2015) in Würzburg, Gemany. As a sponsor of the conference, Kitware presented the fruit of collaboration with Los Alamos National Laboratory on Voronoi and Dendritic meshing:

  • ShaPo: A Framework for Generating 2D Voronoi Meshes was presented by Joachim Pouderoux from Kitware's European offices, Lyon, France.
  • Advances in Dendritic Mesh Generation presented by Mack Kennamond from LANL was co-authored by Tristan Coulange from Kitware's European offices, Lyon, France.
