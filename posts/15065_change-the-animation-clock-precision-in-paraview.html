Starting in ParaView 5.2, there will now be an option to set the precision (number of significant digits) displayed by the animation clock in ParaView. This option is located within ParaView’s Settings/General tab (as in the above screenshot), and can be exposed by selecting the Advanced gear button at the top right corner of the window.

By default, the animation clock displays 17 digits of precision.

By changing this setting, you can set the clock precision to a new value.
