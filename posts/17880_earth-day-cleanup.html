Back in 2013, our headquarters in Clifton Park, New York, adopted part of the street on which our buildings are located. Each Earth Day, we try to head out to clean up the roadside. This year, we had about a dozen employees take part in the cleanup.

Many of us, as well as our neighbors, use this roadside to walk or jog along throughout the year, so it is important to keep it maintained. As Wendell Berry said, “The Earth is what we all have in common.”
