“Triangle Business Journal” has placed Kitware 17th on its 2016 list of cloud computing companies . The journal bases the list on the number of cloud computing employees that companies have in the Triangle Research Park. Kitware currently has six cloud computing employees in Carrboro, North Carolina, and counting. To learn more about the growth of the Carrboro team, please read “ Kitware Celebrates 10 Years and Expands in Carrboro .”
