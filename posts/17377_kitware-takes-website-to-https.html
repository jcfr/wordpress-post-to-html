Late last week, Kitware transitioned its website to HTTPS. The company built the new website on WordPress and designed it to provide visitors with simplified mobile navigation. The website features a layout that more closely aligns with the look of its blog and websites for open-source software solutions such as ParaView, CMake and the Visualization Toolkit (VTK). The company website also condenses information into fewer pages. Kitware encourages members of its user and developer communities to check out the new design.
