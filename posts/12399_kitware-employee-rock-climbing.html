On March 12th, seven employees from Kitware went rock climbing at The Edge of Halfmoon .  There, they learned the basics of safe rock climbing and then proceeded to have a good time, while breaking a sweat. For me, it was especially fun because, at first, I was afraid of falling down and really hurting myself. But, the more I tried, the more I got used to it and conquered my fear. Trust me: I will so be back there to conquer those walls.
