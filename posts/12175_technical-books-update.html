Three of Kitware’s technical books have been recently revised. The ITK Software Guide (ISBN 1-930934-15-7) has been updated for ITK version 2.4, and is 832 pages. The ParaView Guide (ISBN 1-930934-17-3) has been updated for ParaView 2.4, and is 384 pages. Mastering CMake (ISBN 1-930934-16-5) has been updated for CMake 2.4, and is 256 pages.

These books can be ordered directly through Kitware at www.kitware.com or from Amazon.com. Kitware is currently revising The Visualization Toolkit , and The VTK User’s Guide for VTK 5.0. These titles will be available
Summer 2006.
