On behalf of myself, Ken, Bill, Brad, Alex, Zach, Ben and the rest of the CMake team, we are pleased to announce that CMake 2.8.4 is now available for download at: http://www.cmake.org/files/v2.8/?C=M;O=D

It is also available from the usual download links found on the CMake web site: http://www.cmake.org/cmake/resources/software.html
