Kitware is sponsoring the first annual Camp KDE. Camp KDE 2009 is KDE’s first annual developer conference to take place West of the Atlantic. This conference is a by-product of the very successful KDE 4 Release Event held in Mountain View, California. It is intended to ensure that KDE’s presence in the world is not simply seen as being Euro-centric.

Camp KDE 2009 will be held at the Travellers Beach Resort in Negril, Jamaica January 17 – 23, 2009.
