To continue Kitware’s tradition of supporting the needs of our communities, our Clifton Park, NY, and Carrboro, NC, offices are conducting a drive for Ronald McDonald House Charities (RMHC). The drive began last week and continues throughout this week. The drive consists of selling HEARTS to support the program “Help With All Your Heart,” as well as baked goods for those who donate to the cause.

To support the effort, Kitwareans have baked a variety of tasty treats. In addition, the hearts, signed with a name, message, or tribute, are being display in Kitware’s offices. The drive was organized by the Kitware Gives Back team. So far, Kitwareans have raised over $400 for RMHC.

Stay tuned for next week’s blog entry, which will provide the final numbers of the fundraiser!
