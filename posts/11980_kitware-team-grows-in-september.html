Kitware welcomed Jason Li to the team in September. Jason joined the medical imaging team as an R&D engineer at the Clifton Park office. He earned his Master’s degree in computer science from the University of Kentucky at Lexington, and his Bachelor’s degree in mechanical engineering from Beijing JiaoTong University in China. Jason comes to Kitware from Bloomberg L.P. in New York City where he was a C++/C Senior Software Engineer. As part of the infrastructure team, he was responsible for the design and implementation of several internal communication products and analytics tools.
