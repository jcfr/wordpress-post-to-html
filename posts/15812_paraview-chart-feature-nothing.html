Do you deal with Big Data in ParaView? Does showing too many variables when creating a two-dimensional (2D) chart slow you down? We’ve got a new setting for you! In ParaView’s general settings, you can choose to “load no chart variables” by default.

Your charts will come up empty, and you can add variables as needed. This will hopefully save you a bit of time. This feature was requested by a user at Sandia a while back, and migrated from our old bug tracking system to Gitlab. It should be available in the next release of ParaView. Look, no variables:
