Kitware announced today the release of VolView 1.3, a powerful volume rendering system that enables researchers to visualize data from a variety of imaging modalities including CT, MRI, and confocal microscopy. VolView is designed for ease of use, performance, and flexibility at a modest entry price.

New features available in VolView 1.3 include:

  • More data formats including jpeg, png, GE Signa
  • Integrated iso-contours in 2D and 3D
  • Surface area and volume measurements
  • Support for the VolumePro 1000
  • Volume filtering including median, threshold, and smoothing
  • Save volumes and surfaces
  • Application settings saved across sessions
  • Improved lightbox with more layout options
  • Perspective rendering

VolView 1.3 may be downloaded for a free 30-day trial.
