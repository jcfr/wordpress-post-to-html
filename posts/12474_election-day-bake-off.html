Today, we hosted an Election Day Bake-Off!

We had many entries including chocolate-chip cookies, peanut butter cookies, crumb cake, chocolate cake, cookies-and-cream cake, bread pudding, and caramel cupcakes.

After the voting was completed, the winners were determined to be Katie Montembeault's cake in Clifton Park, NY, and Ricardo Ortiz's flan in Carrboro, NC. Congratulations to the winners!
