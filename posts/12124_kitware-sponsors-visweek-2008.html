Kitware is a Gold Sponsor for IEEE VisWeek 2008 which will be held October 19-24, 2008, in Columbus, Ohio. VisWeek 2008 is the premier forum for data and information visualization advances for academia, government, and industry. This event brings together researchers and practitioners with a shared interest in tools, techniques, and technology. The conferences and symposium include exciting and informative collections of workshops, tutorials, papers, panels, demonstrations, posters, and exhibitions.
