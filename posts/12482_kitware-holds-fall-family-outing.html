On October 19, 2014, members of Kitware's New York office participated in a Fall Family Outing at Ellms Farms. It was a cold and windy day, but we all had a great time! The kids (big & small) enjoyed all that Ellms had to offer, including the bounce pillow, the corn maze, a ride on the moo-moo choo-choo, and, of course, cider and donuts!

Kitware's North Carolina office also held a Fall Family Outing in October at Green Acres Farm in Cary. Green Acres offered acres of fun including an amazing maize maze, miniture train rides, pony rides, horse rides, animal feeding, hay rides, and face painting. It was another cold and misty day, but that did not stop us from having fun!
