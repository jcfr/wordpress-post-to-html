Kitware has been awarded a Phase I SBIR by the National Library of Medicine to develop a flexible information visualization and analysis platform for biomedical research. This contracted effort will develop tools in support of ‘omics (genomics, proteomics, metabalomics) research by enabling the visualization of interrelated data in publicly available databases. Kitware is teamed with Michelle Williams from the University of Washington and Thomas O’Connell from the University of North Carolina.
